## Contracts Implemented

### 1. Wanderlust Optimization Route Calculator (`wanderlust-optimization-route-calculator.clar`)

**Purpose**: Plans escapes from routine by creating elaborate itineraries and productivity schedules requiring constant digital infrastructure.

**Key Features**:
- **Destination Management**: Add and track destinations with WiFi quality, co-working density, and infrastructure dependency scores
- **Route Optimization**: Calculate complex wanderlust routes with dependency requirements (47 apps factor)
- **Productivity Scoring**: Track productivity potential based on connectivity and infrastructure requirements
- **User Profiling**: Maintain comprehensive user profiles with optimization preferences
- **Wanderlust Metrics**: Record and analyze optimization metrics including routine escape factors
- **Carbon Footprint Tracking**: Ironically calculate environmental impact while requiring cloud computing

**Core Data Structures**:
- Destinations with detailed infrastructure requirements
- Route calculations with complexity scoring
- User productivity profiles
- Optimization metrics with irony factors

**Notable Functions**:
- `add-destination`: Register new locations with infrastructure scoring
- `calculate-wanderlust-route`: Optimize travel routes with dependency management
- `record-optimization-metric`: Track freedom complexity ratios
- `calculate-wanderlust-score`: Comprehensive destination appeal calculation

### 2. Location-Agnostic Geo-Specific Requirement Manager (`location-agnostic-geo-specific-requirement-manager.clar`)

**Purpose**: Enables working from anywhere by identifying specific infrastructure requirements only available in major metropolitan areas.

**Key Features**:
- **Geographic Infrastructure Mapping**: Track locations with population density and service availability
- **Service Dependency Management**: Map specific service requirements by geographic location
- **Infrastructure Compatibility Verification**: Pre-travel infrastructure validation
- **Contingency Planning**: Emergency backup plans for service disruptions
- **Subscription Optimization**: Manage 12+ subscriptions across multiple time zones
- **Location Independence Scoring**: Ironically score independence through dependency analysis

**Core Data Structures**:
- Location infrastructure with major city status detection
- Service requirements with criticality scoring
- User service profiles with subscription management
- Geo-service compatibility matrix
- Emergency contingency plans

**Notable Functions**:
- `register-location`: Add geographic locations with infrastructure data
- `add-service-requirement`: Define location-specific service dependencies
- `verify-infrastructure-compatibility`: Pre-travel infrastructure validation
- `create-contingency-plan`: Emergency backup planning
- `optimize-subscription-usage`: Cross-timezone subscription management
- `calculate-location-independence-score`: Paradoxical independence through dependence
